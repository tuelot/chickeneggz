2. Scoring & Penalties (The Economy)
💰 Rate: 1 Eggz = R0.10 (10 cents)

• Hit a Baby Chick (🎯 +1 Eggz = R0.10): Successfully hitting a small flying purple cyber-chick awards you +1 Eggz.
• Miss a Shot (❌ -1 Eggz = -R0.10): Firing a stone that completely misses all targets and exits the screen penalizes your balance by -1 Eggz.
• Golden Bird (🌟 +5 Eggz = R0.50): Rare golden birds drop +5 Eggz per hit, multiplied by your current combo.
• Rainbow Bird (🌈 +3 Eggz = R0.30): Rainbow birds grant +3 Eggz per hit, multiplied by your current combo.
• Bomb Bird (💣 -10 Eggz = -R1.00): Hitting a bomb bird instantly wipes 10 Eggz from your balance and resets your combo to zero.
• The Mother Shield (🛡️ -10 Eggz = -R1.00): The large green patrolling Mother Bird acts as a shield for her chicks. Hitting her cracks the shield and costs you 10 Eggz. The on-screen tracking pop-up will show your progress (e.g., MOTHER HIT: 3/10).
